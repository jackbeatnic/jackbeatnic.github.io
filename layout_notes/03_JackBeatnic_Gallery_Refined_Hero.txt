hero_tagline: From the Lens to AI

Akapit 1:
Nature and landscape photographer weaving photography, memory, and imagination into AI-crafted digital art. My lens captures wind-swept seas, ancient forests, mountain peaks, wildlife in quiet light, street scenes, and classic black-and-white details. These moments become the living source for the AI works: Nature Stories — serene minimalist landscapes in flowing layers of deep blues and teals, rich studies of nature, animals, and blooming forms, delicate Flower Stories celebrating fresh femininity, vibrant collages where modern forms meet classical grace.

Akapit 2:
Pure photography keeps its own dedicated space here — unfiltered, real-world moments. Every so often, pencil and pastel find their way in — playful explorations where photography, drawing, and collage begin to meet. Whether generated or shot on location, every piece follows the same pursuit: detail, natural abstraction, light, and the quiet poetry found in between.
